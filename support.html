常见问题

数据存储在哪里？

所有数据存储在你的设备本地，不会上传到云端。

卸载后数据会丢失吗？

是的，卸载应用会删除所有本地数据，请谨慎操作。

如何取消订阅？

打开 iPhone 设置 → Apple ID → 订阅 → 找到鲜花日记 → 取消订阅。

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to the cloud.

Will I lose my data if I uninstall the app?

Yes. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all local data, so please proceed with caution.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Find Flower Diary → Cancel Subscription.
